### Introduction

**The Nida Institute for Biblical Scholarship** serves the global Church at the intersection of Scripture, language, and culture. Named for Eugene A. Nida, we bring together scholars, translators, and practitioners to advance faithful Bible translation and meaningful Scripture engagement across the world’s languages and cultures.

Our work is shaped by a long-standing commitment to the majority world and a single, demanding question: **How can we help the Bible take root, speak clearly, and be lived out within diverse cultural worlds?**

### A Shared Calling

Across all of this work, our aim is the same:
to serve the global Church with resources that are faithful, usable, and shaped by deep respect for Scripture, culture, and community.
